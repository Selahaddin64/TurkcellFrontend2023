Biker

Start your own Motorcycle Club (MC) and roam the open roads as a Biker. Tap into the criminal underbelly of Blaine County and Los Santos with a multitude of off-the-grid businesses, recruiting up to seven Prospects to help you turn a serious profit.

Freemode Basics

Freemode is the live and open world of Los Santos and Blaine County. It's where you'll find everything from mission givers and businesses to social hubs and properties, and much more. Refer to the right-hand side of your Map in the Pause Menu to browse.

Executive

Build your business empire from the ground up through the import and export of illicit commodities, take advantage of exclusive perks to elevate your status above the working-class denizens of the city, and create career opportunities for your friends along the way.

Nightclub Owner

Nightclubs are the beating heart of Los Santos’ nightlife scene. Choose your Club’s location, build its popularity, pick resident DJs, and use your circle of influence to promote your spot as the best in town, all while disrupting your rivals and socializing with friends.

GTA+ Guide

GTA+ is Grand Theft Auto Online’s premium membership program, delivering a series of ongoing and rotating Member-exclusive benefits from across the entire GTA Online experience each month to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S players.

Gunrunner

Dominate the illegal arms trade in Southern San Andreas with Gunrunning: your career as a prolific arms dealer begins with the acquisition of a Bunker from Maze Bank Foreclosures. These underground fortresses will serve as your base of operations for all Gunrunning activities.
